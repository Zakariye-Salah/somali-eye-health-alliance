Community Outreach & Screening — Afgooye

Community-focused outreach materials for markets, health posts and mobile events. Includes radio scripts, poster artwork, and checklists for volunteers.

Key components

  • Event planning checklist
  • Volunteer briefing and consent scripts
  • Market poster and handout designs
  • Short radio spot (30–60 seconds)

Operational tips

  • Coordinate with local clinics for referral slots on outreach days.
  • Set up a visible tent with posters and simple visual acuity check area.
  • Record contact details for follow-up and referral tracking.

Downloads

  • Community outreach toolkit (PDF)
  • Market poster (A2 PDF)
  • Radio spot (MP3)
