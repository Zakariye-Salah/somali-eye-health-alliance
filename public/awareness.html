Awareness Campaigns & Toolkits

Somali Eye Health Alliance produces campaigns, posters and simple toolkits for community health workers, schools and clinics to raise awareness about eye care, preventable blindness, and how to access local services.

School Vision Screening Drive

A complete school screening toolkit — poster, screening form and referral card so teachers and health workers can detect refractive errors early.

Downloads:

  • Screening toolkit (PDF)
  • Poster (A3 PDF)

Community Outreach & Screening

Materials and messaging for outreach events in markets and community centers: oral scripts, poster artwork and community radio briefings.

  • Community toolkit (PDF)
  • Market poster (A2 PDF)

Caregiver & Maternal Health Messaging

Short messages for maternal health clinics about neonatal red reflex checks and when to refer for urgent ophthalmic care.

  • Maternal toolkit (PDF)

Learn more →

Spectacle Acceptance Campaign

Materials to reduce stigma and encourage children and adults to wear prescribed spectacles — includes before/after visuals and community stories.

Surgical Outreach - Patient Information

Patient handouts and consent summaries in simple language, with pre/post op care instructions and transport guidance.

Tele-referral Pilot — Community poster

Poster and short guide for clinics participating in tele-referral pilots to help patients connect with remote specialists.

  • Tele-referral guide (PDF)
  • Clinic poster (A3 PDF)

How to use our toolkits

Most materials are editable, printable and designed for low-bandwidth printing. Toolkits include simple instructions for local translation, resizing for posters, and suggested radio scripts in Somali and English.
