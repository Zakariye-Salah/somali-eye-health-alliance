Caregiver & Maternal Health Messaging

Simple, evidence-based messages and posters for maternal and child health services: neonatal eye checks, signs of urgent referral, and follow-up care guidance.

Includes

  • Neonatal red reflex checklist (one-page)
  • Caregiver poster and leaflet templates
  • Script for brief counselling at immunisation visits

How to use

Use leaflets at ante/post-natal visits, and posters in clinics. Train midwives to perform a red reflex check and refer immediately if abnormal.
