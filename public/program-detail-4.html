Human Resources for Health (HRH)

This program focuses on developing the eye health workforce through training, exchanges and planning.

Project summary

Workforce training, CPD, exchange placements and local trainer development to grow a sustainable eye care workforce in Somalia.

Key activities

  • Training courses & mentorship
  • Exchange placements and scholarships
  • Local trainer development and accreditation

Impact

Increased local surgical capacity, more trained mid-level workers and stronger retention in regional clinics.
