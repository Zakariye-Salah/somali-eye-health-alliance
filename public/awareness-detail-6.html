Tele-referral Pilot — Community poster

Resources to support tele-referral pilots connecting district clinics with remote ophthalmic specialists: posters, referral guidance and simple case recording templates.

Components

  • Clinic poster (A3)
  • Referral form (digital + printable)
  • Short staff checklist for tele-consultations

Downloads

  • Tele-referral guide (PDF)
  • Clinic poster (A3 PDF)

Implementation notes

Use the guide to set up a simple referral workflow, agree times for remote clinics, test internet connectivity and run a small pilot for 4–8 weeks before scaling.
