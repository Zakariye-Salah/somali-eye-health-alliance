Optical Workshop Set-up — Beledweyne

Published: 2025-11-30 — Support establishing a basic spectacle workshop and training staff to measure and fit glasses.

Activities

  • Basic equipment procurement and set-up.
  • Training on refraction, edging and dispensing.
  • Simple bookkeeping & pricing advice for affordability.

Benefits

Local capacity to provide affordable spectacles reduces travel costs for families and improves school attendance.

Contact

For workshop support contact optical@seha.org
