Surgical Mentorship Week — Hargeisa

Published: 2026-06-01 — Visiting specialists mentored local surgeons in safe cataract surgery and theatre protocols.

Focus areas

  • Surgical technique and complication management.
  • Theatre infection control and instrument handling.
  • Pre- and post-operative clinical pathways and counselling.

Impact

Local teams reported improved confidence in complex cases and adoption of standardized checklists across partner hospitals.

Lead trainers

Volunteering ophthalmic surgeons from partner institutions, working alongside local surgical teams.
