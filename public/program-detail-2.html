Health Investment & Finance

This program develops sustainable financing and investment models to strengthen eye health services across Somalia.

Overview

We work with government, donors and local partners to design financing mechanisms, public-private partnerships, and grants allocation models that support long-term service delivery.

Core activities

  • Financial planning for eye units
  • Capacity building in budget & grant management
  • Supporting pilot social enterprise models for sustainability

Outcomes & monitoring

Tracking routine indicators including cost-per-screen, surgical uptake, and local revenue generation for program sustainability.
