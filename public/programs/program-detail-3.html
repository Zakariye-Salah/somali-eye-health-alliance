Research & Innovation

SEHA coordinates operational research, pilots innovative models, and shares evidence to improve eye health service delivery across Somalia.

Focus areas

  • Implementation research for screening & referral
  • Operational pilots (task-sharing, telemedicine)
  • Evaluation and learning

Partnerships

We collaborate with universities, NGOs and local health authorities to embed research into programs and ensure findings inform practice.
