Published: 2026-05-02 — Launch event presenting program aims to translate operational research into scaled interventions.

What is The Bridge Program?

An initiative to connect operational research, pilot testing and program scale-up across SEHA's regions. Launch included partner presentations and pilot planning workshops.

Highlights from the launch

  • Presentations from national partners and academic collaborators.
  • Summary of pilot evaluation framework and monitoring indicators.
  • Calls for partner-led pilots and funding pathways.
