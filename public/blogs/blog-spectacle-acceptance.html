Improving spectacle acceptance

Behavioural and service design tips to improve uptake and sustained use of spectacles in children and adults.

  • Offer options for frames and sizing — ownership matters.
  • Provide counselling for children and parents on the benefit of consistent use.
  • Follow-up reminders and low-cost repair services help retention.
