PONT Project — Improving Access to Oxygen

Published: 2026-03-15 — Partnership with PONT to supply oxygen concentrators, training and protocols to regional hospitals.

Overview

PONT partnered with SEHA to assess oxygen availability in three regional hospitals, supply concentrators and provide clinical and biomedical training so oxygen-dependent patients receive timely care.

Key activities

  • Needs assessment and infrastructure review at 3 hospitals.
  • Procurement and installation of oxygen concentrators.
  • Clinical training for oxygen therapy and neonatal support.
  • Biomedical technician training for maintenance.

Outcomes & impact

  • 3 hospitals equipped with uninterrupted oxygen supply.
  • 25 biomedical technicians trained in maintenance and repair.
  • Improved oxygen availability metrics recorded in the first quarter after installation.

Resources

  • Oxygen therapy protocols (PDF)
  • Biomedical training materials (PDF)

Speakers & partners

PONT (UK), Ministry of Health regional leads, local hospital clinicians and SEHA implementation team.
